What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting digital systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks, unauthorized access, and damage.

It encompasses technologies, processes, and practices designed to protect networks, devices, programs, and data from attack, damage, or unauthorized access.

Key Goal: Maintain the security and integrity of information systems while ensuring business continuity
